GLOBAL WARMING

Global warming is the long term rise in Earth's average temperature,primarily caused by human activities like burning fossil fuels and deforestation,which release greenhouse gases such as carbon dioxide. This leads to several consequences like melting glaciers, rising sea levels, extreme weather events and threats to ecosystems and human health.

CAUSES OF GLOBAL WARMING

Human Activities:

The primary driver is the release of greenhouse gases from burning fossil fuels for energy , industrial processes and transportation.

Deforestation:

Trees absorb carbon dioxide,so cutting them down reduces the planet's ability to remove this gas from the atmosphere.

EFFECTS OF GLOBAL WARMING

Climate change:

This include more frequent and intense heatwaves, droughts,floods and storms.

Healths impacts:

Extreme temperatures can cause health problems like heatstrokes and change in weather patterns can contribute to the spread of diseases.

SOLUTIONS OF GLOBAL WARMING

Renewable energy:

transitioning to souces like solar and wind power reduces reliance on fossil fuels.

Afforestation and reforestation

Panting more trees is crucial for absorbing carbon dioxide from the atmosphere.
